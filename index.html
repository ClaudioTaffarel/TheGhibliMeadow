About

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation studio created in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, together with producer Toshio Suzuki. Known for its breathtaking hand-drawn animation and deep storytelling, the studio has produced some of the most beloved films in cinematic history.

The company gained worldwide recognition with works such as My Neighbor Totoro, Princess Mononoke, and the Academy Award-winning Spirited Away. Each film is filled with memorable characters, stunning visual worlds, and themes that touch on humanity, nature, and imagination.
